How to Navigate

We want to keep our website decreet in order to maintain our highly selective elite customer base. Only those who are registered art bidders can bid for these customers!

If you are a customer, please contact one of our registered art bidders to look through our catalog of rare artwork!

If you are a registered art bidder, click on the Log-in button above to get started!

Once you get past the log-in page, you can click on each of the individual art pieces to see which one you'd like to bid on, and you can only bid higher than the current bid price, which will be displayed below the artwork. From this page, you only have 3 minutes to get bid on everything you want! So act fast and you must know which artworks you want before you enter!
